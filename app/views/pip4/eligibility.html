2. Eligibility

To get a Personal Independence Payment, you must:

  • be aged 16 to 64
  • be in Great Britain when you claim, unless you're a member of the Armed Forces or their family
  • be free from immigration control or a sponsored immigrant
  • live in the UK, Ireland, Isle of Man or the Channel Islands - or, depending on your circumstances, in another EEA country or Switzerland

Your health condition or disability doesn't have to be permanent but must be expected to last for at least 12 months in total. It must affect your ability to do at least one of the following:

  • prepare food, eat or drink
  • wash, bathe or go to the toilet
  • dress yourself
  • read or write
  • take medicine or treatments
  • manage your money
  • get on with other people
  • go out or move around unaided
