4. Apply

To apply you'll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • details of any doctors, carers, counsellors or other people who help you
  • to give details of how your condition affects your daily life

You can send evidence such as care plans or written diaries of your condition to support your claim after you've applied.

If you're terminally ill and have a DS1500 from your doctor phone 0800 917 2222 to have your claim dealt with faster.

If you already get Disability Living Allowance you'll be told when to apply for Personal Independence Payment (PIP) - don't apply until you're asked to.

You can save your application and return to it at any time.
