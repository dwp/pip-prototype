Upload medical information

You can upload electronic copies of medical information that helps explain your condition, medication and treatments. If you don't have electronic copies you can post them after you apply.

Upload your:

  • medical reports - from doctors, occupational therapists and other medical experts
  • care or treatment plans
  • medical test results
  • hospital discharge letters
  • outpatient clinic letters
  • current prescription lists
  • statement of special needs
