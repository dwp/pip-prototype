Money and bills

Describe what affect your condition has on how you manage your money and household bills.

Do you have difficulties with money, eg knowing how much things cost or counting your change?
Does your condition cause you difficulty paying or managing bills?

This includes if you forget to pay on time, or aren't sure whether you have enough money or how to pay.

Does someone help you pay bills or manage your money?
