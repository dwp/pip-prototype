Leaving your home

We need to understand if your condition affects you when you're:

  • getting out and about
  • mixing with people

Include as much information as you can in your answers, such as:

  • if you can do all of the activity, or if you can do some parts but not others
  • if you can do it as often as you need to, or if it varies depending on what kind of day you're having
  • how long it takes you
  • if you risk hurting yourself or having an accident when you're doing it
  • how you feel during and after you've done it
