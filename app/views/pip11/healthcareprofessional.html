Professionals who know about your condition

To help us get all the information we need to make a decision, you can include up to 5 professionals who will share details about the effect of your condition with us (or a company working on our behalf).

They could be a doctor, counsellor, teacher, key worker or anyone else who has information about the effects of your condition.
