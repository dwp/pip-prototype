Getting dressed

  • Does your condition affect you getting dressed?

    This includes if you need to wear clothes that are easy to put on or fasten, if you have difficulty choosing suitable clothes, or if you rely on someone to encourage you or help you get dressed.

    or

    What do you have difficulty with?

    Select as many as you need.

    What to include in your answer

    Include as much information as you can, such as if you:

    • risk hurting yourself or having an accident when you’re getting dressed
    • get any side-effects from getting dressed
    • can get dressed as often as you need to, or if it depends on what kind of day you're having
    What to include in your answer

    Include as much information as you can, such as:

    • roughly how long it takes you to get dressed
    • if you can do some parts but not others
    • if you rely on any aids or adaptations
    • if you rely on someone to help you, supervise you or encourage you to get dressed
