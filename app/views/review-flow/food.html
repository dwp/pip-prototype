Your day-to-day activities - preparing food

Do you use any aids or appliances when making a meal?

This could include things like easy grip utensils or single lever taps. Also include if you can only use a microwave instead of a hob or cooker.

Describe what they are and how often you use them.

Do you need help from another person to make yourself a meal?

As well as physical help this includes if you need them to supervise you or remind you how to prepare or cook something.

Describe the help you need and how often you need it.

Describe how often you have 'good' and 'bad' days and what happens during them.
