Dressing and undressing yourself

  • How often are you able to dress and undress yourself?
  • How often do you need to use an aid or appliance to dress or undress?

    Suitable aids could include modified buttons, modified zips, front-fastening bras, modified trouser, velcro fastenings or shoe aids

  • How often do you need prompting to get dressed or undressed?
  • How often do you need help choosing appropiate clothing?
  • How often do you require assistance to dress or undress your lower body?

    By lower body we mean help putting on and taking off your trousers, socks, skirts, tights, underwear or shoes

  • How often do you require assistance to dress or undress your upper body?

    By lower body we mean help putting on and taking off your t-shirt, jumper, bra, coat, shirt or top

Please tell us more information about the difficulties or help you need to get dressed or undress

Help for this part of the form

Tell us more about:

  • If you can't do this safely, to an acceptable standard, as often as you need to and in a reasonable time.
  • If you need help from another person, tell us what kind (for example whether they need to remind or motivate you to dress or undress, plan the task for you, supervise you while you are doing it, physically help you or prepare your clothes for you).
  • If you use aids and appliances, tell us what type they are and how you use them.
  • If your ability to do it varies, tell us in what way and how often - for example, telling us about good and bad days or how it varies throughout the day.
  • If you can do it but it takes you a long time
  • if you have side effects from carrying out the activity - either during or after the activity - like pain, breathlessness or tiredness.
