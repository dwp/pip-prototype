Your day-to-day activities - preparing food

Do you need any help to prepare a simple meal from fresh ingredients?

This includes food preparation and being able to safety cook or heat food.

Do you use an aid or appliances to prepare or cook a simple meal?

For example, perching stools, lightweight pots and pans, easy grip handles on utensils, single lever arm taps or liquid level indicators.

Do you need help from another person to prepare or cook a simple meal?

Help includes someone physically assisting you, cooking food for you or supervising you to make sure you are safe.

Do you need someone to prompt, encourage, and remind you to cook or how to do so?

Do you need help peeling, chopping food or opening a can?

Can you safely cook or heat food on a cooker hob or in a microwave oven?
