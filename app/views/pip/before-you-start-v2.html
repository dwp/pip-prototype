Before you start

To get Personal Independence Payment you must have a long-term health condition or disability that:

  • affects your mobility, physical ability or mental health
  • makes it difficult for you to live independently
  • you've had for 3 months
  • is expected to last at least another 9 months

Your application may include a face to face meeting with a healthcare professional to assess how your condition affects your daily life.

If you're terminally ill and have been given less than 6 months to live then you should phone 0800 120 1234 to have your claim dealt with more quickly.
