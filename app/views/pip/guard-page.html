Step 1

Your day-to-day activities

  • Do you need help or special equipment to make a simple meal, eg to peel vegetables or heat food?
  • Do you need help to eat or drink, or need reminding to eat?
  • Do you need to monitor or treat your health condition at home?

    This includes taking medication, tracking changes in your condition or treatments such as home dialysis.

  • Do you need help washing or bathing?
  • Do you need help going to the toilet?

    This includes if you're incontinent or can't use a normal toilet without special equipment.

  • Do you need help or reminding to get dressed or undressed?

    This includes choosing what clothes to wear.

  • Do you have difficulty talking to people or understanding what they've said to you?
  • Do you need help reading or understanding basic symbols or signs?
  • Do you find it difficult to get on with people?
  • Do you need help with money, eg counting your change or knowing what you've spent?
  • Do you have any anxiety or stress about being outside your home, eg about catching a bus?
  • Do you need help or aids to move about, eg a walking stick?
