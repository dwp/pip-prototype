3. Make a claim

To apply you'll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • details of any healthcare professionals you've seen or who have been involved with your care
  • to give details of how your condition affects your daily life

The application can take up to 2 hours.

If you already get Disability Living Allowance then wait until you get a letter telling you to apply for PIP.

If you're terminally ill and have a life expectancy under 6 months you can get your claim processed faster by phoning 0800 917 2222.
