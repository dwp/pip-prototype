Your day-to-day activities - preparing food

Do you need any help to prepare a simple meal for one from fresh ingredients?

This includes food preparation such as peeling, chopping or opening a can, and safely cooking or heating food on a cooker hob or in a microwave oven.

Do you use an aid or appliances to prepare or cook a simple meal?

For example, perching stools, lightweight pots and pans, easy grip handles on utensils, single lever arm taps or liquid level indicators.

Do you need help from another person to prepare or cook a simple meal?

Help includes someone physically assisting you, cooking food for you, supervising you to make sure you are safe, and prompting, encouraging or reminding you to cook food or how to do so.

Do you have any side effects from carrying out the activity - either during or after the activity?
