1. Overview

Personal Independence Payment (PIP) is between £21.55 and £138.05 a week. This is to help you manage the needs you have because of your disability or health condition.

You can still get PIP while you're working and it doesn't affect other benefits you or your partner get.

If you already get Disability Living Allowance you'll be told when to apply for PIP - don't apply until you're asked to.
